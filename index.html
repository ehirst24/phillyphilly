About Me

Hey everyone! I've worked at Power for almost 2 years now and in that span I've had experience in Customer Development, Inbound Scheduling, and currently Inside Sales. Before working at Power I went to Temple University where I graduated with a degree in Engineering Technology. I am originally from a smaller city in upstate New York called Binghamton, but have lived in Philly for 7 years now... and don't worry guys, I'm a birds fan. My most recent jobs prior to working at Power were painting houses, construction installing solar panels on a solar farm, and I interned at a CNC milling plant; what a combo to lead me on this path to coding!

I enjoy traveling and experiencing all sorts of new activities. My hobbies include snowboarding, hiking, and recently I've been dabbling in photography and upping my cooking game. I'm also a bit of a movie buff, be sure to add me on letterboxd. I'll follow back!

I look forward to meeting you all and if you have any questions feel free to reach out
